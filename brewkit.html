Description

In this kit, you'll have all you need to achieve artisan-homebrew-making-legend status.

  • Brewers yeast. CHECK.
  • Pressure release cap. CHECK.
  • Sterilising powder. CHECK.
  • Easy-to-follow instructions. CHECK.
  • Invisible-but-very-real sense of smugness. DOUBLE CHECK.

Our ingenious kit contains a bottle lid with a pressure release system that helps prevent the bottle becoming over pressurised.

You can use fruit juice, soda or make your own flavours. Takes around a week to brew.

You won't find the bottles, but take a chill pill sis, all you need is a standard 2 litre soda bottle and you'll find we've included all the instuctions for you before you embark upon your noble homebrew quest.

The kit itself might not look fancy, but we can promise you'll have a shedload of fun cooking up a storm, and the results are, frankly, just hippy dippy dude.

The standard kit has 3 caps and valves and enough yeast and cleaner to make around 40 bottles of home brew.
